Commands:

!enable-corruption [character-name]|(true|false)
    -> Enables/Disables corruption data on character
!enable-weapon-corruption [character-name]|[weapon-name]|(true|false)
    -> Enables/Disabled corruption data on weapon of character
!set-corruption [character-name]|(true|false)
    -> Triggers corruption mode.
!next-turn
    -> Triggers next turn
